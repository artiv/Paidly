Why you might just need Paidly?

How simple it all is:

    1. Choose who you want to pay
    You can choose to pay someone from your phone's contacts list, someone with whom you have had a previous e-transaction, or even someone from Nairobi who only has a bank account. Everything just works.
    2. Enter the amount you want to pay
    Pay in your local currency, in the receiver's currency. Our system handles everything else for you.
    3. Drool over the simplicity of all this
    Click the pay button and you are done.

How are you different from say, Paypal?

Think along the lines, paying anyone, anywhere, and you will see the difference. For starters, however, the ones you want to pay need not know about Paidly let alone having a merchant account setup with us.
