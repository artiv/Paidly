Why you might just need Paidly?

  • 1. You have got better things to do than standing in long, long queues.
  • 2. You are patient but not particularly fond of filling in 11 digit bank codes, OTPs more than one time, and the your DNA sequence from the 13th chromosome.
  • 3. You want it to be as simple as 1,2 and 3.

How simple it all is:

    1. Choose who you want to pay
    You can choose to pay someone from your phone's contacts list, someone with whom you have had previous e-transaction, or even someone from Nairobi who only has a bank account. Everything just works.
    2. Enter the amount you want to pay
    Pay in your local currency, in the receiver's currency. Our system handles everything else for you.
    3. Drool over the simplicity of all this
    Click the pay button and you are done.

Just how safe is it going to be?

Paidly is going to be built on the same technologies which power the best of websites the world over. Military grade encryption with SSL/TSL and multi-factor authentication mean you can rest assured that your accounts will never be compromised with.
